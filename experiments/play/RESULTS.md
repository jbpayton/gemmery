# The played game (post-hoc explainability)

A full Gnosia-style game played with the store live, then interrogated after
the fact: WHY did we vote that way in round 2? Every answer reconstructed
from the DAG alone — beliefs at that commit, consumed evidence edges, the
branches not taken. Write-up: [WHY_WE_PLAYED.md](WHY_WE_PLAYED.md); artifact:
`browser.html`, `play_dag.png`.
